Symbiotic Systems: the Connection Between the Ocean and the Human Body

The ocean and the human body, seemingly disparate entities, share a profound connection. Both are complex, self-regulating systems that are essential for sustaining life. The ocean covers over 70% of the Earth's surface and is a vast ecosystem that supports an incredible diversity of life, while the human body is a intricate network of organs and systems that work in harmony to maintain our health and well-being.
